The biggest kaleidoscope ever was shown at the Expo 2005 in Japan.
     It was called the "Earth Tower".
     Sunlight came through a spherical glass-ceiling and was led by a complex formation of mirrors
     to three rotating coloured glass discs which created beautiful, kaleidoscope-typical patterns.
